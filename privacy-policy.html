Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

NeoAtum Inc ("we", "our", or "us") operates PatentFlow, an AI-powered patent drafting service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your intellectual property. This policy describes our practices regarding the collection and use of your information.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and contact information (email address)
  • Payment information (processed by Paddle.com)
  • Account credentials and preferences
  • Communication records with our support team

Invention and Technical Information

When you use our service, we collect information about your inventions and technical concepts that you input for patent draft generation. This information is treated with the highest level of confidentiality.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our service, including IP addresses, browser type, device information, and usage patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI patent drafting service
  • Process your invention information to generate patent drafts
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security and Encryption

We implement end-to-end encryption to protect your intellectual property and personal information.

Our security measures include:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Regular security monitoring and incident response procedures

Your invention details are encrypted before being processed by our AI models and remain encrypted during storage.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or invention details to third parties, except as described in this policy.

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Payment Processing: Payment information is processed by Paddle.com as our payment processor
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with continued privacy protection)

6. AI Model Training and Data Use

We may use aggregated and anonymized data to improve our AI models and service quality. This process involves:

  • Removing all personally identifiable information
  • Anonymizing invention details to prevent identification
  • Using only general patterns and structures for model improvement
  • Never using specific invention details in model training

Your specific invention information is never shared with external AI model providers or used in ways that could compromise your intellectual property.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after closure
  • Invention Data: Retained for the duration of your subscription and up to 90 days after cancellation
  • Generated Patents: Available for download during your subscription period
  • Payment Records: Retained as required by law and our payment processor

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at akmarketing2045@gmail.com.

9. International Data Transfers

Our service may involve transferring your information to countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for European users and CCPA for California residents.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
