Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legally valid?

PatentFlow generates patent drafts that follow standard legal formats and requirements. However, we recommend consulting with a patent attorney for final review and filing, as patent law can be complex and jurisdiction-specific.

Is my data secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption to protect your intellectual property. Your invention details are encrypted during transmission and storage, and we never share your data with third parties.

What happens after the 2-year offer?

PatentFlow is currently in pre-launch development. The $2,500 pre-order grants you 2 years of unlimited access once the full platform launches. After your 2-year period ends, your account will automatically transition to the Pro plan at $299/month. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account settings. All pre-order funds go directly toward building and scaling the full version of PatentFlow.

Does this use human experts?

No. PatentFlow is a fully automated AI-powered system. There is no human legal review from NeoAtum Inc unless explicitly stated. Our AI models are trained on extensive patent databases to provide accurate drafts.
