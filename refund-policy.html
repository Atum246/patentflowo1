Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

At PatentFlow, we strive to provide exceptional service through PatentFlow. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds and cancellations of our AI-powered patent drafting service.

All refund requests are processed through PayStack.com, our payment processor and Merchant of Record. Paddle provides all customer service inquiries and handles returns.

2. Refund Eligibility

Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within 7 days of the initial purchase or renewal, provided:

  • You have not generated more than 3 patent drafts
  • You have not downloaded any generated content
  • The refund request is made within the 7-day window

Special 2-Year Offer

The special 2-year offer ($2,500) is eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase, provided:

  • You have not generated more than 5 patent drafts
  • You have not downloaded any generated content
  • The refund request is made within the 14-day window

Team and Enterprise Plans

Team and Enterprise subscriptions are eligible for a prorated refund within 14 days of purchase or renewal, subject to usage limitations similar to monthly subscriptions.

3. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be provided in the following circumstances:

  • Requests made after the applicable refund period
  • Accounts that have exceeded the usage limitations
  • Violations of our Terms of Use
  • Requests for partial refunds on annual or multi-year plans after the refund period
  • Dissatisfaction with AI-generated content quality (as results may vary)
  • Changes in business needs or circumstances

4. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at akmarketing2045@gmail.com
  2. Include your account email and reason for the refund request
  3. Provide your PayStack transaction ID if available
  4. Allow up to 5 business days for review and processing

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed through PayStack.com and typically take:

  • Credit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 7-14 business days

5. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting support. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Important: Cancellation does not automatically qualify you for a refund. Refunds are subject to the terms outlined in this policy.

After cancellation, you will retain access to your account and generated content until the end of your current billing period.

6. Service Credits

In cases where a full refund is not applicable, we may offer service credits at our discretion. Service credits can be applied to:

  • Future subscription payments
  • Plan upgrades
  • Additional features or services

Service credits are non-transferable and expire 12 months from the date of issuance.

7. Technical Issues and Service Interruptions

If you experience significant technical issues or service interruptions that prevent you from using PatentFlow, we may provide:

  • Extended subscription periods to compensate for downtime
  • Service credits for affected periods
  • Priority support to resolve issues quickly

Please report technical issues promptly to ensure we can provide appropriate compensation.

8. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision, you may:

  • Request a review by contacting our support team
  • Provide additional information or documentation
  • Escalate the matter through Paddle.com's dispute resolution process

We are committed to resolving disputes fairly and promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website.

Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
